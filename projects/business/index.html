Zarmina … a tidally-locked world thought to lie within the circumstellar habitability zone of Gliese 581, a red dwarf star in the Libra constellation. But is Zarmina real? And can man survive there? One hundred volunteers leave Earth in 2068 to find answers to these questions. They’ll struggle to survive a forbidding climate, dangerous flares, sickness, near starvation and attacks by local wildlife. No white knight will come to their rescue; the colonists will have to fend for themselves.
